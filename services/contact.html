Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What services does Younghub IT Solutions offer?

Younghub IT Solutions offers a wide range of services including Digital Marketing, Website Development, and Graphic Designing. We deliver affordable, fast, and secure solutions within a week through our wide network of professional freelancers, tailored to meet each client’s specific needs.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and requirements of the project. A simple website can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while more complex websites with custom functionality may take 3-4 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is flexible and based on the scope and requirements of each project. Website development packages start from just NPR 2,000 up to NPR 80,000. For Graphic Designing and Digital Marketing services, please contact us for customized and affordable packages. We offer a free consultation to understand your needs and provide the best quote within your budget

Do you offer maintenance services after project completion?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support services for all our projects. We have various maintenance packages available to ensure your website or application remains up-to-date, secure, and functioning optimally.

How do I get started with Younghub IT Solutions?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us through our contact form, email, or phone. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and budget. After understanding your needs, we'll provide you with a detailed proposal and timeline.
